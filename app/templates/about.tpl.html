THE eXCHANGE

This is a space where we can connect with others to truly engage with what it means to be the body of Christ through reading of the Word and living it out.

We study scripture, share meals, worship, and learn to live purposefully in our neighborhoods, workplaces and campuses. The eXCHANGE is also a place where you can be honest, and where you can be fully known. We look to the New Testament Church in the book of Acts as an exammple of a vibrant community living their lives together as they met in the temple and in their homes.

As we study the Word, we grow as disciples of Jesus. We realize that we all can uniquely serve the church and the world. We all have a special role to play.
